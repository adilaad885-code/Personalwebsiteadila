About Me

📽 My go-to Movies!

Picture Title Summary
Mr. And Mrs. Smith

A high energy action comedy about a married couple John and Jane Smith, who are secretly assassins working for rival organizations. They are unaware of each other’s secret identities until they are assigned to eliminate each other. As their professional and personal lives collide, the couple navigates explosive action, comedy and romantic tension, ultimately rediscovering their love while taking down their enemies.
Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha

A heartwarming romantic comedy set in a seaside village. Yoon Hye-jin, a pragmatic dentist from the city, moves to the village of Gongjin and meets Hong Du-sik, a charming, jack of all trades local handyman. Through humorous and touching interactions with the villagers, Hye-jin learns the value of community, friendship and love. Besides its also leading to a sweet romantic development between her and Du-sik.

Bride Wars

A comedy about two lifelong friends, Liv and Emma, who become rivals when they discover their weddings are booked on the same day at the same prestigious New York City venue. Their friendly competition escalates into hilarious sabotage as each tries to outdo the other. Amidst the chaos, they must confront their own egos and the strain on their friendship, ultimately learning lessons about love, friendship, and forgiveness.
